About 


PM Shree Govt. Model Sanskriti Sr. Sec. School, Muana , run by the state government and provide free education to students up to the secondary level under the Haryana Government. It has a well-structured curriculum and qualified teachers who aim to provide quality education to students. The state government has also initiated various schemes and programs to improve the infrastructure and facilities in these schools. It shows some great progress from past years and provide great result under the guidance of teachers.
